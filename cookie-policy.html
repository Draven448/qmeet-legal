Cookie Policy

Understanding how and why we use cookies and tracking technologies to improve your experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device. They help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and allow our app to function correctly. We also use similar technologies like web beacons, pixels, and mobile identifiers.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Essential for the website to function (e.g., logging in, security). Cannot Opt Out

2. Functional Cookies

Enhance functionality and personalization (e.g., language settings, theme). Optional

3. Performance & Analytics

Help us understand how users interact with QMeeet to improve performance. Optional

4. Marketing & Advertising

Show relevant ads and measure performance. Optional

Managing Your Preferences

In-App

You can manage your cookie preferences directly in the QMeeet app:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Select Privacy
  3. Choose Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Check your browser's "Privacy" or "Security" settings.

Mobile Settings

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads

Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact us at privacy@qmeet.com.
