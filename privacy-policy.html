Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

At QMeet, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with Google Play Store Data Safety requirements, GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA.

1. Information We Collect (Data Safety)

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service. This aligns with the Google Play Data Safety section:

Personal Information

  • Name: For user identification.
  • Email Address: For account authentication and communication.
  • Phone Number: For account verification (SMS).
  • Date of Birth: To verify age (18+) and calculate age range for matching.

Location Data

  • Precise Location: Collected only with your explicit permission to show potential matches nearby.
  • Approximate Location: Derived from IP address for general region analytics.

Financial Information

  • Payment Methods: Processed via third-party secure payment processors (e.g., Google Play Billing, Stripe). We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Purchase History: Records of subscriptions and in-app purchases.

Photos and Videos

  • Profile Photos: User-uploaded images for profile display.
  • Shared Media: Photos or videos sent in chat (encrypted).

Device and Usage Data

  • Device ID: For analytics and fraud prevention.
  • App Activity: Interactions, matches, likes, and time spent.
  • Crash Logs: To identify and fix stability issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following specific purposes:

  • App Functionality: To create accounts, enable matching, and facilitate chat.
  • Account Management: To verify identity and manage subscriptions.
  • Safety and Security: To detect fraud, block spam, and enforce community guidelines.
  • Analytics: To understand user behavior and improve app performance.
  • Communication: To send service updates and, with consent, marketing emails.

3. Third-Party Sharing and Service Providers

4. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have specific rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion: Request the permanent deletion of your account and data.
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data in certain scenarios.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent for features like Location or Push Notifications at any time via device settings.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active to provide the service.
  • Account Deletion: Upon request, your account enters a 30-day recovery period. You can restore it during this time.
  • Permanent Deletion: After 30 days, your personal data is permanently deleted from our active databases. Backups may retain encrypted residual data for up to 90 days before being overwritten. You may contact us after this period to request confirmation of erasure.
  • Legal Retention: We may retain transaction records for tax and legal purposes for a longer period as required by law.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Strict Age Restriction: QMeet is actively restricted to users aged 18 and older.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or 18).
  • If we discover that a user is under 18, we will immediately freeze the account and permanently delete all associated data.
  • We employ age verification measures during registration by requiring all users to input their date of birth and confirm they are 18 or older.

7. Data Security & International Transfers

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (using TLS/SSL) and at rest.
  • Access Control: Strict access controls limit internal access to user data.
  • International Transfers: If we transfer your data to a country other than your own (specifically outside the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK), we ensure it is protected by appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to opt-out of the "sale" of personal information. QMeet does not sell your personal information in the traditional sense. However, for full transparency under CCPA:

  • Do Not Sell My Personal Information: To exercise this right, please email us directly with the subject line "CCPA Opt-Out".
  • Dedicated Request Email: donotsell@qmeet.com

We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights.

9. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience and analyze traffic. For detailed information, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. For significant changes, we will provide additional notice (e.g., via email or an in-app notification).
