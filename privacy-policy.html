Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

At QMeeet, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account and use QMeeet, you provide us with:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, date of birth, phone number
  • Profile Information: Photos, bio, interests, preferences, location
  • Verification Information: Documents for identity verification (if applicable)
  • Payment Information: Billing details for premium subscriptions
  • Communications: Messages, chats, and other interactions with other users

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the service, features used, time spent
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address or precise location (with permission)
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Data collected through cookies, pixels, and similar tools

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Create and manage your account, show your profile to potential matches
  • Personalize Experience: Recommend compatible matches based on preferences
  • Communication: Send notifications, updates, and marketing messages
  • Safety and Security: Verify identities, detect and prevent fraud, enforce our terms
  • Improve Service: Analyze usage patterns, develop new features
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. How We Share Your Information

With Other Users

Your profile information, photos, and activity are visible to other users. Your messages are visible to users you communicate with. You control what information appears on your profile.

With Service Providers

We share data with third-party vendors who help us process payments, host and maintain our servers, provide customer support, conduct analytics, and deliver advertising.

For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information when required to comply with legal obligations, protect the safety of users or the public, or investigate potential violations of our terms.

4. Your Privacy Choices

  • Account Settings: Edit profile, control visibility, manage notifications.
  • Marketing: Opt out of promotional emails via "unsubscribe".
  • Location Services: Disable in device settings (may limit features).
  • Cookies: Manage in browser settings.

5. Data Data Retention & Security

We retain your information as long as your account is active. We implement encryption (in transit and at rest), regular security audits, and access controls to protect your data.

6. Children's Privacy

QMeeet is strictly for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we learn we have collected information from a child, we will delete it promptly.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@qmeet.com
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@qmeet.com
