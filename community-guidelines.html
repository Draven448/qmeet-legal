Prohibited Content & Behavior

Harassment and Abuse

We have zero tolerance for threatening, intimidating, or bullying behavior. Hate speech based on race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or disability is strictly prohibited.

Inappropriate Content

Do not post nudity, sexually explicit content, graphic violence, illegal activities, or promote drug use.

Deceptive Practices

Catfishing, impersonation, creating fake profiles, scamming, or soliciting money is banned.

Consequences of Violations

Depending on the severity and frequency of violations, we may interpret actions as needing:

  • Warning: First-time minor violations.
  • Content Removal: Deletion of violating content.
  • Account Suspension: Temporary restriction from using QMeeet.
  • Permanent Ban: Permanent removal for severe/repeat violations.
  • Legal Action: Reporting illegal activity to law enforcement.
